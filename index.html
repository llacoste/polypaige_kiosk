About Paige

Paige Ahern is the creative mind behind PolyPaige, a whimsical world of handcrafted acrylic jewelry and art. Blending playful design with artistic depth, Paige transforms everyday inspiration into wearable joy. With a background in fine arts and a love for quirky expression, she brings imagination to life one brooch, necklace, and doodle at a time.
